title Cross Connect Test
description Two diagonal tracks that cross in the middle

# First diagonal track - goes from lower-left to upper-right
# Angle 45 degrees
new 45
gen cabs 1 cars 3 speed 8 every 15
str x 5
track1: str x 10
str x 5
bin

# Second diagonal track - goes from upper-left to lower-right
# Angle -45 degrees (315)
# Positioned to cross track1 in the middle
new 315
gen cabs 1 cars 3 speed 6 every 20
str x 5
track2: str x 10
str x 5
bin

cross connect $track1 $track2
